Neighbors

Designed and created a web application for community coordination to improve upon current Home Owner Associations. By bringing visualization, accessibility and connectivity to our local communities, neighborhoods can handle mediation and management with far more transparency.

Autonomous Wall-Following Car - TI MSP432 LaunchPad

Coded and constructed this autonomous car to maintain a distance from a wall as well as evade obstacles. The bot uses Pulse Width Modulation to power it's differential drive. With optical encoders we were able to implement feedback controllers to maintain wheel velocity equilibrium. The same concept was applied in the use of ultrasonic sonar sensors to maintain a wall following distance.

Autonomous Vehicle - Arduino Robot Controller

An autonomous robot that can navigate through a space with obstacle avoidance. With an ultrasonic sonar sensor attached to a servo motor, the robot is able to view 180 degrees in front of the bot with only one sensor. In addition, the robot uses a "Dead Reckoning" style of odometry and a light sensor to locate points of interest making him a flexible and adaptive autonomous robot.
